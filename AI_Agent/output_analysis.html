Here is our comprehensive Business Analysis and Systems Architecture review of the proposed project.

Functional Requirements

  • Real-time driver tracking: update driver location on a map every 10 seconds
  • Automatic notifications:
    • When the driver starts the trip (SMS and Email)
    • When the driver is within 2 kilometers of the delivery address (Push notification to the customer's mobile app)
    • When the delivery is completed or if there's a delay
  • Secure tracking data: ensure driver location data is highly secure and only accessible when the driver is active on a shift
  • Power efficiency: ensure the tracking system does not excessively drain the driver's phone battery

Non-functional Requirements

  • Performance: ensure the tracking system updates in real-time without significant delays or latency
  • Security: ensure all tracking data is encrypted and protected against unauthorized access
  • Usability: ensure the tracking system is easy to use and understand for both drivers and customers
  • Compatibility: ensure the tracking system is compatible with standard Android devices

Risks

  • Technical difficulties: potential issues with real-time tracking, notification systems, or data security
  • Driver acceptance: potential resistance from drivers to being tracked in real-time
  • Customer expectations: potential mismatch between customer expectations and the actual performance of the tracking system
  • Timeline: potential delays in rolling out the new feature within the desired timeframe

Assumptions

  • Assuming the existing logistics application has a robust infrastructure to support real-time tracking and notifications
  • Assuming the drivers will have standard Android devices with necessary permissions and settings to support the tracking system
  • Assuming the customers will have the latest version of the mobile app to receive push notifications

Questions to Client

  • Can you provide more information about the current logistics application infrastructure and its ability to support real-time tracking and notifications?
  • How will the drivers be notified and trained on the new tracking system and its requirements?
  • What are the key performance indicators (KPIs) for the success of the new feature, and how will they be measured?
  • Are there any existing APIs or integrations that need to be considered for the tracking system and notifications?
